### 3.3 Clear Space & Minimum Size

**Clear Space:**
[DIAGRAM: Logo with clear space marked]
Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of [logo element] on all sides

**Minimum Sizes:**

Print:
- Full logo: 0.75" (1.9 cm) wide minimum
- Icon only: 0.5" (1.27 cm) minimum

Digital:
- Full logo: 120 pixels wide minimum
- Icon only: 32x32 pixels minimum

### 3.4 Logo Colors

**On White Background:**
Use full-color primary logo

**On Light Backgrounds:**
Use full-color primary logo
Ensure sufficient contrast

**On Dark Backgrounds:**
Use reversed (white) logo version

**On Photography:**
Use white logo with dark overlay (40-60% opacity)
OR place logo in low-detail area of image

### 3.5 Incorrect Usage

❌ DO NOT:
- Stretch, skew, or distort the logo
- Rotate the logo
- Change logo colors
- Add effects (shadows, gradients, outlines)
- Place on busy backgrounds without overlay
- Recreate or alter logo elements
- Use below minimum size
- Violate clear space requirements

### 7.2 Tone Guidelines

**Customer Support:**
Tone: Empathetic, helpful, patient
Example: "I understand how frustrating this must be. Let me help you resolve this right away."

**Marketing:**
Tone: Confident, inspiring, benefit-focused
Example: "Transform your workflow with our innovative solution."

**Technical Docs:**
Tone: Clear, precise, instructional
Example: "Click the 'Settings' icon in the top-right corner."

**Social Media:**
Tone: Friendly, conversational, timely
Example: "Great question! Here's how it works..."

### 7.3 Writing Style

**Do:**
- Use active voice
- Write in second person ("you")
- Use contractions in casual contexts
- Be concise
- Use sentence case for headings

**Avoid:**
- Jargon without explanation
- Passive voice
- Exclamation point overuse
- ALL CAPS or Title Case overuse

**Grammar:**
- Oxford comma: yes
- One space after periods
- Em dashes for emphasis
- En dashes for ranges
