# Measuring Communication Effectiveness

This guide covers how to measure and improve your internal communications through metrics and regular audits.

## Key Metrics to Track

### Engagement Metrics
- Email open rates (target: >60%)
- Click-through rates (target: >20%)
- Newsletter subscription rates
- Slack reaction counts
- Meeting attendance rates
- Wiki page views

### Comprehension Metrics
- Questions asked after announcements
- Support ticket volume related to comms
- Survey scores on clarity
- Rework due to miscommunication
- Time to decision/action after communication

### Sentiment Metrics
- Employee satisfaction with communication (survey)
- Trust in leadership communication
- Feeling of being informed
- Communication as factor in engagement scores

### Efficiency Metrics
- Time spent writing communications
- Time from draft to publish
- Channels used per message type
- Redundant communications sent

## Communication Audit Process

### Quarterly Review

**1. Inventory all communication channels and cadences**
- What are we sending?
- How often?
- To whom?

**2. Assess effectiveness**
- What's working well?
- What's creating noise?
- What's missing?

**3. Gather feedback**
- Survey employees
- Review metrics
- Conduct focus groups

**4. Make adjustments**
- Consolidate redundant comms
- Add missing communications
- Change frequency or format
- Sunset ineffective channels

**5. Document and communicate changes**
- Share what you learned
- Explain changes
- Set new expectations
